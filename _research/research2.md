I was selected for the [QiDi program](https://news.tongji.edu.cn/info/1002/82199.htm) at Tongji University and served as a key member on the project under the guidance of [Prof. Peng Qi](https://cse.tongji.edu.cn/6b/68/c15581a158568/page.htm) . Our project was rated as a national innovation project under the China National University Student Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Program. I was primarily responsible for robot perception.

 ![demo](/images/AeroEye-demo.png)

 I used YOLOv5 to identify cracks and locate them.  
 ![image](/images/005.jpg)
 
 As the first author of a student, I have published two invention patents, which are in the substantive review stage.  
 Patent1 introduces a method for detecting aircraft engine cracks based on YOLOv5.  
 Patent2 introduces obstacle avoidance method, system, device and storage medium for aircraft engine defect detection robot.
