I am a third year undergraduate student from [College of Electronic and Information Engineering](https://see-en.tongji.edu.cn/index.htm), [Tongji University](https://www.tongji.edu.cn/). Additionally, I am an exchange student in the [Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/eee/) at [The Hong Kong Polytechnic University](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/en/) for the fall semester of 2024. My research interest includes robot perception, computer vision, deep learning and human-computer interaction.

I am fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Rui FAN](https://www.ruirangerfan.com/) from [Machine Intelligence and Autonomous Systems(MIAS) Group](https://mias.group/) at Tongji University and [Shanghai Research Institute for Intelligent Autonomous Systems (SRIAS)](https://srias.tongji.edu.cn/main.htm). My research focuses on road damage detection and autonomous driving. Additionally, I have worked under the guidance of [Prof. Peng QI](https://cse.tongji.edu.cn/6b/68/c15581a158568/page.htm) at Tongji University, where I played a key role in a national innovation and entrepreneurship project for college students aimed at developing a robotic system for aircraft engine damage detection.
